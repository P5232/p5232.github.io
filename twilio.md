<h3>Text Notification Service Description</h3>
The text notification service provides its users with SMS notification of important events or reminders. This could include situations such as school cancellation or closings.  

<h3>Opt-In Policy</h3>
Keyword: OPT-IN. The keyword is found in a text message sent to the customer. Once the customer texts the keyword, they are provided a double opt in where they are asked to Reply "OPT-IN" to confirm they would like to receive promotional SMS. To Opt-In, the customer provides their cell phone number and receives and initial text from the number 1-866-314-0113. Another method to join the text notification service is to email P5232Blog@gmail.com with your full name and cell phone number. The customer is then given the option to OPT-IN or STOP.

Sample text opt-in message sent to the customer:
"This number was provided to opt-in to the text notification service. Reply with "OPT-IN" to confirm or "STOP" to be removed."

<h3>Opt-Out Policy</h3>
Keyword: STOP. The users can stop the service at any time by replying with the keyword "STOP" or "OPT-OUT". One final confimation message will be sent to confirm that the number was removed.

Sample text opt-out message sent to the customer:
"This number requested to be removed from the text notification service. If this was in error, reply "OPT-IN" to rejoin. Otherwise, consider this number removed."
