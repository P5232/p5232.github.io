Golden Latte

INGREDIENTS
  • 1.5 cups almond milk
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup

    • Heat in a saucepan and whisk until it starts to boil and then serve in your favorite mug.
