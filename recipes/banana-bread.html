Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 stick of butter (softened)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch of salt (if butter is unsalted)
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 eggs

Baking Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Butter or grease a 9"x13" glass casserole pans then add flour.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients first with the sugar and then add the dry ingredients.
  4. Bake for approximately 45-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out cleanly.

Gluten-Free Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1/3 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1-1/2 cups of gluten-free all-purpose flour with xantham gum
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs

Baking Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Butter or grease a 9"x13" glass casserole pans then add flour.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients first with the sugar and then add the dry ingredients.
  4. Bake for approximately 45-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out cleanly.
