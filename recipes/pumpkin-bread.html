Pumpkin Bread Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 3.25 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 cups of pumpkin
  • 1 cup of vegatable oil
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 cups of sugar
  • 2/3 cut of water
  • 4 eggs

Baking Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Butter or grease a 9"x13" glass casserole pans then add flour.
  3. Bake for approximately 45-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out cleanly.
